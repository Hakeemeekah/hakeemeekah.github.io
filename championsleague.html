About Champions League

The UEFA Champions League is Europe's premier annual club football tournament, featuring top teams from various national leagues that qualify through their domestic performance or by winning previous European competitions. The competition recently adopted a new 36-team league phase format for the 2024–25 season, replacing the previous 32-team group stage, and concludes with knockout rounds culminating in a final match at a neutral venue. Real Madrid is the most successful club, with a record 15 titles.
